CPAN modules for dealing with the Japanese language

These CPAN modules assist with programming problems involving the Japanese language.

Lingua::JA::Moji handle characters of various kinds.

Text::MeCab break Japanese text into individual words.

Lingua::JA::Numbers Convert a number into Japanese.

Lingua::JA::FindDates scan text and extract year, month, and day.

DateTime::Calendar::Japanese::Era Japanese era dates and western year conversion.

Lingua::JA::Yomi Convert English into Japanese katakana.
